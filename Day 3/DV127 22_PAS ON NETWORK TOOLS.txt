22_SAS ON NETWORK TOOLS


Q1. What is cable crimping?
A. Crimping is a process used to join wire to wire or wire to terminal. 


Q2. What is a modular connector?
A. The modular connect is the concept of a new type of plug-in connector for the connection of electrical cables, fiber optic cables. 


Q3. What is a wifi analyzer?
A. A Wi-Fi analyzer tool helps you track and analyze your wireless network’s performance with ease. 


Q4. What is a tone generator and probe?
A. The tome generator and probe can be used to verify cable continuity, identify wiring faults, determine line polarity and voltage in network and modular telephone lines.


Q5. What is a punch down tool?
A. Punch down tools are used to terminate wires into punch down patch panels and punch down keystone jacks.


Q6. What are cable testers?
A. A cable tester is an electronic device used to verify the electrical connections in a signal cable or other wired assembly. 


Q7. What is a loopback plug?
A. A loopback plug is a connector used for diagnosing transmission problems on parallel and serial ports.
